About Me

Words are mywildest art.

I'm Samridhi — a poet, storyteller, and creative writer from Hyderabad, India. Writing for me isn't a skill, it's a compulsion: a way to make sense of the world through rhythm, imagery, and raw honesty.

As a B.E. student at MVSR Engineering College and a published blog writer, I bring both analytical precision and lyrical depth to every piece I craft. My work spans introspective poetry, social commentary, flash fiction, and narrative prose — always grounded in emotion, always reaching for something true.

Whether you need a wedding poem that makes guests weep, a birthday verse that feels impossibly personal, or a creative piece that carries weight — I write it with care.

✦ Featured Work

Breathe, My Dearest

It is a famous line, inhale the future, exhale the past. Let me tell you this mantra of mine. Let me recite to you, a new year's resolution to keep in mind. Inhale the divine purity, exhale all insecurities. Inhale the bliss of life, exhale the agony of death. Inhale a compassionate spirit, exhale the thorns of cynicism. Inhale the cultivation of optimism, exhale the growth of pessimism. Inhale the desire of knowledge, exhale the bitterness of judgment. Inhale the duality of joy and sorrow, because life without either is an uncertain tomorrow.
— Samridhi Narwade
